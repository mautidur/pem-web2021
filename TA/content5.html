Val d'Isère
France
Home to many ski slopes and schools, including the French National Ski School.

Most Rellevant Review

What can you say about a place like Val D'Isere? A gem among ski resorts. High enough to be pretty much guaranteed with snow Nov-May, but low enough for it to still be a genuine village and not a purpose built resort. The ski area is vast ,not just Val D, but also Tignes as well. There are lovely runs all over the place, and ( a bonus for beginners) you can get a cable car down from the learning area. The snow making is amazing , the lifts are fast and comfortable and the ski schools ( especially Oxygene) are brilliant with newbies. It feels a bit like a genuine village though clearly it has expanded massively over the years. Can be a bit posh and therefore expensive...but pick your bars and restaurants carefully and you'll be fine.

-OrrDublin
