Mergui Archipelago
Myanmar
Mergui Archipelago located in far southern Myanmar (Burma) and is part of the Tanintharyi Region.

Most Rellevant Review

I have to say, the Mergui Archipelago is a strangely beautiful place. A spectacular beach from afar is, upon closer inspection, wears a necklace of debris and aside from the random bird or butterfly sighting, no tangible sense of fauna. The waters are deserted except for the swarms of local fishermen in their curious, sinister seeming boats trawling thru the waters at all hours. Looking like cast-offs from "Mad Max at Sea" these precarious machines are bizarre from afar, disturbing up close. Yet, the chef on our chartered 90' yacht, the 'yar Aventure, would frequently stop these boats in hopes of buying a fresh catch for dinner. Amusingly, almost every time, beer, not cash, was the preferred form of payment.

-lisabinnyc
