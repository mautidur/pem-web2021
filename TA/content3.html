Icefield Parkway
Canada
The Icefields Parkwayis a 230 km (140 mi) long scenic road that parallels the Continental Divide

Most Rellevant Review

Words can't describe how beautiful the drive is. Every minute you see amazing views all around, you just can't take in the beauty. So many places to stop to take photos and stunning waterfalls and the Glacier you can climb on way up to Jasper. Allow a day so you can go slowly and same on return trip. Take plenty to drink and snacks as nowhere really to stop off and have plenty of fuel. Travelled all over world and this was one of highlights for us and best thing we did on 3 week trip to Canada

-EleanorBristol
